🥛 Doodh Peene Ki Dua (دعاء شرب اللبن)

**Bismillah-hir-Rahman-ir-Raheem**

When drinking milk, it is Sunnah to recite the following dua to seek barakah and gratitude for Allah’s blessings.

Arabic:

اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَنَا فِيهِ وَزِدْنَا مِنْهُ

Transliteration:

Allahumma baarik lanaa feehi wa zidnaa minhu

Translation:

O Allah, bless it for us and give us more of it.

🌙 Benefits

  • ✅ Following Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ
  • ✅ Asking Allah for barakah and increase in rizq
  • ✅ Expression of gratitude towards Allah

🤔 Why Recite This?

Because life without milk is like Resident Evil without zombies – dry, pointless, and sad. 😂

📝 Article Summary

Milk is a complete nourishment gifted by Allah. Drinking it with the dua not only fulfils our body’s need but also uplifts our soul by following Sunnah. Remember, Prophet Muhammad ﷺ loved milk and taught us to ask Allah for its barakah and increase. May Allah grant us the ability to act upon Sunnah in all small deeds of life. Ameen.
